FAQ

Common questions

What order statuses does ShahoSuite support?
ShahoSuite supports four order statuses: Draft (created, not confirmed), Completed (fulfilled), Cancelled (voided) and Refunded (payment returned). Each is colour-coded for instant readability.
Can I search and filter orders?
Yes. Search by order ID, customer name or description. Filter by status — All, Draft, Completed, Cancelled or Refunded.
Does ShahoSuite show order revenue totals?
Yes. The summary bar shows Total Orders, Pending Orders, Completed Orders and Total Revenue — always live and up to date.
