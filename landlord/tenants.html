Tenant Module

One place for tenant lifecycle, notes, statements and compliance

ShahoSuite gives landlords a clean tenant dashboard, automatic rent status, digital lease references, and property-linked tenant histories. Simplify notices, access requests, and GDPR-ready records with one UK-focused landlord module.

Tenant profiles

Store tenant details, rent history, documents and notes for every tenancy in a searchable portfolio.

Lease lifecycle

Manage active agreements, renewals, move-in and move-out dates from one landlord-friendly screen.

Rent tracking

See due dates, payments, arrears and statements at a glance so you can act quickly.

Compliance reports

Why it works
