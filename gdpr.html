Data Isolation — Your Data Is Only Yours

Every ShahoSuite account is completely isolated from every other account. There is no way for one business to see another business's data.

🏠
Account Isolation
Your customers, invoices, orders and staff are completely separate from all other ShahoSuite accounts. No shared data pools.
🔐
Authenticated Access Only
All data requires authentication to access. No anonymous access, no public endpoints exposing your data.
🔒
Encrypted Storage
All data stored using AES-256 encryption at rest on Azure. Even physical access to servers cannot read your data.
🚫
No Data Sharing
Your data is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising. It exists solely to run your ShahoSuite account.

Your Rights Under UK GDPR

👁
Right to Access
Request a full copy of all data we hold about you and your business. We respond within 30 days.
✏️
Right to Rectification
Request correction of any inaccurate personal data we hold. Most data can be corrected directly in Settings.
🗑️
Right to Erasure
Request deletion of your data at any time. Cancel your account and all data is deleted within 30 days.
📤
Right to Data Portability
Export all your data as CSV from any module at any time. Your data is always yours to take.
🚫
Right to Object
Object to processing of your personal data in certain circumstances. Contact us at admin@shahosuite.com.
