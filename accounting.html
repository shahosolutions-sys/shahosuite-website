FAQ

Common questions

What accounting features does ShahoSuite include?
ShahoSuite includes a General Ledger, Bills & Subscriptions tracking and an Accounts Receivable view — all with a real-time Profit & Loss summary auto-calculated from your invoices and orders.
Does ShahoSuite auto-calculate income from invoices?
Yes. Every invoice marked as paid automatically updates your income in the General Ledger. No manual entry, no re-keying — your accounts are always current.
Can I export my accounting data?
Yes. ShahoSuite lets you export your full accounting data as a CSV file at any time — filtered by date range, category or type. Ready for your accountant or HMRC submission.
Is ShahoSuite a replacement for my accountant?
No — and we want to be clear about that. ShahoSuite keeps your records organised and accurate so your accountant has clean, complete data to work from. It reduces the time they spend on your books, saving you money.
Is my financial data safe?
Yes. All accounting data is stored on Microsoft Azure UK data centres, SSL encrypted and fully GDPR-compliant. Your financial records always belong to you.
