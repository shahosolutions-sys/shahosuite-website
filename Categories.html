FAQ

Common questions

What does the Categories module do?
Categories lets you organise all your products into named groups. Each category shows total products, stock value and status — making your inventory easier to manage and your analytics more meaningful.
Can I archive categories I no longer use?
Yes. Archive moves inactive categories out of view while keeping all linked products and history intact. Toggle Show Archived to bring them back any time.
Do categories appear in Analytics?
Yes. The Analytics module shows Income by Category and Expenses by Category — driven directly by your category structure.
