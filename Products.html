FAQ

Common questions

What product information does ShahoSuite store?
ShahoSuite stores product image, SKU, name, description, category, selling price, cost price, stock level and status (Active/Archived) — all in one organised record.
Does ShahoSuite alert me when stock is low?
Yes. The Products page flags Low Stock items automatically — highlighted in the stock column and counted in the summary bar so you always know what needs restocking.
Can I export my product list?
Yes. Click Export CSV to download your full product catalogue any time — ready for accountants, stock takes or external systems.
