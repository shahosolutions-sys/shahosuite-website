FAQ

Common questions

What does the ShahoSuite Analytics module show?
ShahoSuite Analytics shows total revenue, orders in the last 5 days, your top customer, active customer count, a monthly revenue bar chart, a daily orders line chart, income breakdown by category and expense breakdown by category — all in real time.
Is the analytics data real-time?
Yes. The dashboard updates automatically as invoices are paid, orders are placed and customers are added. You can also click Refresh to pull the latest data instantly, and filter by time period.
Can I export my analytics data?
Yes. Click Export CSV from the Analytics dashboard to download your full data — ready for your accountant, business review or HMRC records.
Is my analytics data safe?
Yes. All data is stored on Microsoft Azure UK data centres, SSL encrypted and fully GDPR-compliant. Your business data always belongs to you.
How much does ShahoSuite Analytics cost?
The Analytics module is included in the Director account at £50/month with all 16 modules. Staff accounts are £15/month each. See full pricing.
