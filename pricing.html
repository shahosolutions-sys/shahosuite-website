FAQ

Pricing questions

Is everything really included in £50/month?
Yes. The Director account at £50/month includes all 16 modules — Invoicing, CRM, Booking, Accounting, Analytics, Credit Notes, Orders, Suppliers, Categories, Send Message, Support, Staff, Settings, Audit Log, Products and Dashboard. No modules are locked behind higher tiers.
How do I add staff members?
The Director invites staff from the Staff module — enter their name and email, they receive an invitation and create their login. Each staff member costs £15/month. You can add unlimited staff members.
Are there any per-invoice or per-booking fees?
No. ShahoSuite has no per-invoice, per-booking or per-transaction fees. One flat monthly price covers unlimited customers, invoices, orders and bookings.
Can I cancel at any time?
Yes. No contracts, no minimum term, no cancellation fees. Cancel any time from your account settings and your subscription ends at the next billing date.
Is there a free trial?
You can create an account and explore ShahoSuite before committing. Sign up at shahosuite.com/register.
Is my data safe?
Yes. All data is hosted on Microsoft Azure UK data centres — SSL encrypted, GDPR-compliant and backed by a 99.9% uptime SLA. Your data always belongs to you.
