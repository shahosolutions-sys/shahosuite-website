FAQ

Pricing questions

How do I get access?
Apply at app.shahosuite.com/register. We review every application personally and respond within one business day. Approved clients receive a 14-day guided demo and onboarding. There is no instant open signup.
Is everything included in £50/month for Wholesale?
Yes. The Wholesale Director account at £50/month includes all wholesale modules — CRM, products, orders, invoices, online shop, accounting, analytics, staff and more. No modules locked behind higher tiers.
Is landlord access charged separately?
No. Landlord operations use the same account pricing: Director £50/month and Staff £15/month each.
How do I add staff members?
The Director invites staff from the Staff module — they receive an email invitation and create their login. Each staff member costs £15/month. Unlimited staff seats available.
Are there per-invoice or per-transaction fees?
No. ShahoSuite has no per-invoice, per-order or per-transaction fees. One flat monthly price per account.
Can I cancel at any time?
Yes. No contracts, no minimum term, no cancellation fees. Cancel from account settings — subscription ends at the next billing date.
Is there a free trial?
There is no instant self-signup trial. Approved clients receive a 14-day guided demo with personal onboarding so you explore the platform properly before committing.
Is my data safe?
Yes. All data is hosted on Microsoft Azure UK — SSL encrypted, GDPR-compliant, 99.9% uptime SLA. Your data never leaves the UK.
