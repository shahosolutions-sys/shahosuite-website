FAQ

Common questions

Can customers book appointments themselves online?
Yes. ShahoSuite gives you a customer-facing booking page where people can choose the service, staff member and available time slot — from any device, at any time. No phone calls needed.
Does ShahoSuite send automatic booking confirmations?
Yes. The moment a booking is made, a confirmation email is automatically sent to the customer. Reminders can also be sent before the appointment — you configure when, ShahoSuite handles the rest.
Can I manage multiple staff members' availability?
Yes. Each staff member has their own availability schedule set up in the Staff module. Bookings are automatically assigned to the right person and the calendar reflects their live availability. No double-bookings possible.
Is ShahoSuite booking suitable for salons and clinics?
Yes. ShahoSuite is used by salons, beauty studios, clinics, therapists, tutors, personal trainers, consultants and tradespeople across the UK. Any business that books appointments by time will find it fits immediately.
Is my customers' booking data safe?
Yes. ShahoSuite is hosted on Microsoft Azure with UK data centres, SSL encryption and full GDPR compliance. Customer booking data is stored securely and you always own your data.
How much does ShahoSuite booking cost?
The Booking module is included in the Director account at £50/month — along with all 15 other modules. No per-booking fees, no setup costs, no contracts. Staff accounts are £15/month each. See full pricing.
