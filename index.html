The UK business management software that replaces 10 separate tools

Running a small business in the UK means juggling invoicing software, a CRM, a booking system, an accounting package, and more — each with its own monthly fee and login. ShahoSuite changes that. We built a single, all-in-one business management platform specifically for UK small businesses, freelancers, agencies, and service providers — so you can manage every part of your business from one place, at one flat price.

Unlike HubSpot or Zoho, which charge per user and scale costs unpredictably, ShahoSuite costs £50 per month flat — full access to all 16 modules with no contracts.
