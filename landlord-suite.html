Everything included in Landlord Suite

Modules grouped by how UK property teams work: portfolio, compliance, rent collection, tenant communication and day-to-day operations.

Main

📊Dashboard

Your portfolio command centre — occupancy, rent collected, arrears, open maintenance and expiring certs at a glance.

Highlights: Financial pulse, rent collection strip, smart alerts with one-click links, first-run checklist, tenant portal link.

Automated: cert, lease, Section 13 and referencing alerts on every open.

🏘️Properties

Full portfolio — lettings, HMOs and voids with rooms, photos, legal landlord details and compliance dates.

Options: Postcode lookup, EPC, Gas Safety, EICR, HMO licence, room management, up to 5 photos, Ready to Rent status.

📄Leases

Create assured shorthold tenancies, generate PDF summary, email to tenant, track deposit protection, Section 13 and renewals.

Options: AST / Periodic / Fixed Term, deposit scheme (TDS, DPS, MyDeposits), rent due day, break clause.

Automated: rent records per month, lease expiry alerts, optional portal invite after create.

🏷️Sales & Buy

Track property deals from evaluation to completion on list or kanban board.

Stages: Evaluating → Under Offer → Exchanged → Completed → Withdrawn.
Money

💷Rent Tracker

Month-by-month rent collection — paid, partial or overdue. Record payments, chase arrears, email receipts with your bank details.

Options: Month navigator, KPIs, bulk mark paid, overdue chase panel.

Automated: tenant rent reminders (3 days before, due day, 3 & 7 days overdue) + daily arrears digest to landlord.

📒Accounting

UK tax-year income and expenses per property — with Section 24 mortgage interest relief. Pro plan.

Options: Tax years, expense categories, bank CSV import, tax report CSV, per-property yield.

Automated: rent payments logged as income; inventory purchases can log as furniture expenses.
