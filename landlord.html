Full Suite Overview for UK Landlords

Dashboard

Your portfolio at a glance

The moment you log in you see four live KPIs and quick-access controls that keep your portfolio moving: occupancy, arrears, expiring compliance certificates, and open maintenance jobs.

Occupancy Rate

What percentage of properties are currently let, colour-coded green/amber/red so you can spot gaps instantly.

Total Arrears

The exact pound amount of unpaid rent for the month. Zero means all rent has been collected.

Expiring Certs

How many safety certificates are due within 60 days, so nothing catches you off guard.

Open Jobs

Outstanding maintenance jobs across your portfolio, with fast access to the property and job details.

Quick access and live property lists

Below the KPIs you get buttons to every key page and a live property list showing address, type and arrears status — all clickable so you can move from overview to action in one click.

Properties Group

Property, tenant and lease management

Everything your landlord business needs to manage portfolio, tenants, tenancies, and property status from one UK-hosted platform.

🏘️ Properties

Add address, postcode, property type, number of bedrooms, monthly rent and status (To Let / Let / Under Maintenance). For HMO properties, record shared space notes for kitchens, bathrooms and communal areas.

👤 Tenants

Keep a full tenant record with name, email, phone, move-in/move-out dates, status, photo and room number for HMOs. View complete financial statements with rent history, partial payments and running balance.

📄 Leases

Every tenancy agreement in one table. Record property, tenant, tenancy type, dates, rent, deposit and room number with colour-coded expiry warnings.

Lease actions

Each lease row includes Edit, Agreement and Remove actions. The system generates a rent record automatically when a new tenancy is created.

Rent Group

Rent Tracker

The operational heart of the suite. See every property-tenant combination for the selected month with payment status, summary totals and easy chase tools.

✅ Paid

Green badge for confirmed payments so you know which rents are fully collected.

🟡 ½ Partial

Amber badge showing partial payments and how much remains outstanding.

⚠️ Due

Rent expected but not yet received. The system highlights these records so you can act quickly.

Void

Properties with no active tenant this month are shown separately so you can manage void periods clearly.

One-click payments and filter focus

Mark rent paid with a click or enter partial amounts inline. Filter by Due or Partial records to focus on the accounts that need chasing.

Maintenance Group

Maintenance Jobs & Compliance

Track repairs, inspections and safety documents alongside your rental and property workflow.

🛠️ Maintenance Jobs

Log jobs with property, description, priority, status, reported date and contractor notes. Open job counts feed straight into the dashboard.

📁 Documents & Certs

Store gas safety, EPC, EICR, PAT, fire risk and other certificates. Track expiry dates and see status badges for Valid, Expiring Soon and Expired.

Compliance made visible

Expiry-driven colour badges mean you never miss a certificate renewal. Compliance status is always clear across your portfolio.

Finance Group

Accounting

Track every rent receipt, repair cost, insurance payment and expense by property. See each address' financial performance and your total portfolio position.

💰 Income & Expenditure

Log rent, repairs, mortgage, insurance, agent fees and more. Everything is categorised by property.

Portfolio performance

See how each property contributes to your bottom line, plus the wider portfolio result for tax and reporting.

Inspections Group

Viewings / Inspections

Schedule viewings and inspections with property, date, contact, type, status and outcome notes. Upcoming entries appear on the property detail panel.

📅 Viewings

Book and manage property viewings for vacant units with a clear schedule and status tracking.

🔎 Inspections

Record periodic inspections for occupied properties with notes and outcomes, then review them from the property dashboard.

Communication Group

Support

Raise and track support tickets directly in the platform. Questions, billing issues and technical problems are all logged in one thread with full history.

System Group

Settings

Update business profile, branding, subscription, user preferences and notification settings. Company name, logo, address and contact details appear on generated tenancy agreements and tenant statements.

How to get started

Get up and running fast

Step 1 — Add your properties

Go to Properties in the left menu. Click "Add Property" and fill in address, postcode, property type, bedrooms and monthly rent. For HMOs, add the property once and assign rooms separately.

Step 2 — Add your tenants

Go to Tenants. Click "Add Tenant" and enter full name, email, phone, property and room number for HMOs. Search by name, email, phone or property.

Step 3 — Create a lease

Go to Leases. Click "Add Tenancy", link tenant and property, set dates, rent, deposit and tenancy type. The tenant status updates automatically.

Step 4 — Track rent

Check Rent Tracker for paid, partial and due records. Mark rent as paid with one click and spot arrears quickly on property cards.

Step 5 — Log maintenance

Go to Maintenance Jobs and add a job with property, issue, priority and contractor notes. Update status as it progresses.

Step 6 — Upload certificates

Go to Documents and Certs. Upload gas safety, EPC, EICR and other compliance documents. The system tracks expiry dates and flags renewals.

Step 7 — Review finance

Open Finance for rent collected, outstanding amounts and maintenance costs across your portfolio.

Step 8 — Book viewings

Use Viewings to schedule inspections and appointments. Past and upcoming bookings are listed separately for clear planning.
