FAQ

Common questions

What does the ShahoSuite Dashboard show?
The Dashboard shows Today's Revenue, Today's Bookings, Overdue Invoices, Low Stock Items, a 12-month Revenue Overview chart, Quick Action shortcuts and Smart Suggestions — all live and updated automatically.
What are Smart Suggestions?
Smart Suggestions analyse your business data and surface the most important actions — overdue invoices that need chasing, stock items that need reordering, and other priority tasks. They update automatically as your data changes.
Can I create invoices and bookings directly from the Dashboard?
Yes. The Quick Actions row has five shortcuts: New Invoice, New Order, New Customer, New Product and New Booking — one click to start any of these common tasks without navigating to a separate module.
